h1. Hermaphroditic C. elegans


<img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-283" title="home-photo" src="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/b/be/CrawlingCelegans.gif" alt="" width="700" />

The hermaphroditic Caenorhabditis elegans connectome is the only connectome published to date.  Thanks to the herculean efforts of White et al. (1986), and many others since, C. elegans has become a model organism, not just in the standard sense, but also in setting the bar for data sharing and open research.
